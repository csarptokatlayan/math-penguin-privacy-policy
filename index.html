Privacy Policy for Math Penguin

Effective Date: November 2025

Math Penguin is developed and published by CopperHand Studio, an independent developer located in Türkiye, operating under the Third Axis Games creative label. Unless otherwise stated, all intellectual property in the game (including visuals, sound, text, and code) belongs exclusively to CopperHand Studio.

1. Overview

Math Penguin is a 100% offline, ad-free educational game designed for children. The app does not collect, transmit, or store any personal or non-personal user data. The game does not require an account and does not connect to any online services or third-party SDKs.

3. Data Collection & Processing

We collect no data of any kind. Specifically:

  • No personal identifiers (e.g., name, email, age).
  • No device or diagnostic data.
  • No analytics or crash reporting.
  • No gameplay telemetry or usage statistics.

The game operates entirely offline. No information is sent to external servers.

4. App Permissions

The app requests no device permissions (e.g., no internet, storage, camera, microphone, contacts, location, or notifications). If platform defaults require any manifest entries, they are unused by this app and do not access data.

5. Advertising, Links, and Purchases

  • No advertisements of any kind.
  • No external links, webviews, or social media buttons.
  • No in-app purchases and no subscriptions.
  • No third-party SDKs/APIs (including analytics, ad, or attribution SDKs).

6. Children’s Privacy & Families Policy

The game is designed for children and complies with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the Google Play Families Policy. Because no data is collected, there is no risk of exposing personal information. Game content is age-appropriate and contains no user-generated content, chat, or online features.

7. Intellectual Property & AI-Assisted Assets

All game materials (images, sounds, music, and text) were created by CopperHand Studio. Some assets were produced with the assistance of AI tools licensed for commercial use (e.g., music and image generation systems). CopperHand Studio reviews, edits, and finalizes all such assets and asserts full ownership and distribution rights for in-game use.

We do not use third-party copyrighted materials without authorization, and we do not incorporate user content.

8. Security

Because the app does not collect or transmit data, the risk of data breach or unauthorized access is effectively eliminated. The game contains no network features.

9. Offline Operation

Math Penguin runs completely offline. Network connectivity is not required for installation or gameplay, and the app will not attempt to connect to the internet.

10. Updates to This Policy

If future features ever require internet connectivity or any form of data processing, this policy will be updated before such changes go live, and in-app disclosures will be provided where appropriate.
