Privacy Policy for Math Penguin

Effective Date: October 2025

Thank you for playing Math Penguin, a fun and educational mobile game designed for elementary-school-aged children. Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected while you or your child uses the Math Penguin application.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information such as names, email addresses, home addresses, phone numbers, or any form of personally identifiable data.

However, to improve gameplay and understand how our game is being used, we collect non-personal device usage data through Unity Analytics. This may include:

  • Basic device information (e.g., device type, operating system version)
  • Game performance metrics (e.g., level progress, session length, crash reports)
  • Anonymous usage statistics (e.g., how often the app is opened, which features are most used)

All of this data is collected in aggregate form and cannot be used to personally identify any individual user.

2. How We Use the Collected Information

The non-personal data collected via Unity Analytics helps us to:

  • Improve gameplay balance and performance
  • Identify and fix bugs or technical issues
  • Understand general usage patterns to enhance user experience

We do not sell, trade, or share any collected data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Advertising

Math Penguin may contain advertisements to support free access to the game. These ads are served in compliance with child-friendly and family-safe standards. No targeted advertising is displayed, and no personal information is used to personalize ad content. The ads shown are based solely on generic context and not on user behavior or identity.

4. Children’s Privacy

Our app fully complies with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar global data protection regulations. Since we do not collect any personal data, parents and guardians can be confident that their children’s privacy is protected at all times.

5. Data Security

All data collected through Unity Analytics is transmitted securely and stored in accordance with Unity’s own privacy and data protection policies. We take reasonable steps to ensure that this information remains protected and is not accessed or altered by unauthorized parties.

6. Third-Party Services

Math Penguin uses Unity Analytics as a third-party service. You can learn more about Unity’s data collection and privacy practices by visiting their official privacy policy:

https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any updates will be posted on this page, and the “Effective Date” will be revised accordingly.
